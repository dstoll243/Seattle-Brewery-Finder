Cloudburst Brewing

Just weeks after Elysian Brewing was purchased by Anheuser-Busch, veteran brewer Steve Luke left his long time position with Elysian to branch off and start Cloudburst Brewing. Cloudburst Brewing sports a no-frills brewery and taproom in the heart of Seattle, serving as a landing spot for beer aficionados to try some of the most well crafted beers the city of Seattle has to offer. Cloudburst offers a limited selection of pre-filled growlers, otherwise you will only find their beers on tap at various establishments.

Peddler Brewing

The Peddler Brewing Company began with Haley and Dave Keller's love of brewing in March 2013. They now have 15-barrel brewing system to create and market their fantastic selections available at local stores, restaurants, and of course, their brewing house. Peddler prides itself in making it feel welcoming to all their customers by providing offers special day activities to bring your families and pets. Additionally, the Peddler are active advocators of cycling, including providing discounts for Cascade card members and a small bike repair stop.

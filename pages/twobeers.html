Two Beers Brewing Company

10 years into their existance, Two Beers Brewing is still one of the most consitent breweries that you can find in Seattle. Working out of an office park in the Sodo/Georgetown neighborhood, you will find a great spot for groups of all sizes with 24+ taps of Two Beers, plus sister Seattle Cider Company, as well as food prepared on site by Bread & Circus's. Defintiely one of the better options from one of the longer tenured Seattle brewing venture.
